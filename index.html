Mother's Day Breakfast and Brunch Popup Meal

Belgian Waffles

Tasty cultural experience! This brunch main are waffles, topped with fresh fruit and whip cream. Comes with a side of avocado and egg on toast. The drinks are coffee and fresh orange juice.

Breakfast Croissants

The main course for this Mother's Day Brunch is a fresh baked croissant with spinach, egg, honeyed ham and aged cheddar. It will come with sides of home fries and fruit salad. Drinks include coffee, English Breakfast Tea and fresh orange juice.

Classic Breakfast

This family style breakfast comes with a platter of scrambled eggs, peameal bacon, toast and hashbrowns. Drinks are included as well.

Quiché

This lovely quiché is filled with all sorts of good things! Bacon bits, green peppers, mushrooms and more make it the perfect way to start the day. It comes with a side of fresh croissants and fruit salad. Drinks include coffee, tea, orange juice or maybe even a hot chocolate with whipped cream.
